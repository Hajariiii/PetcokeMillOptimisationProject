About the solution

With this interface, you can create precise predictions for the Petcoke Mill's outputs. We created a robust XGBoost MultiRegression model takes into consideration the most important variables impacting the mill's performance. 

Furthermore, this interface includes an optimization feature allowing to improve the Petcoke Mill's performance and efficiency. We can fine-tune the mill's characteristics and operating settings to get optimal outcomes by using Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO). The goal of this optimization procedure is to improve the mill's overall productivity, energy efficiency, and cost-effectiveness.

This interface enables you to exploit the information produced from the Petcoke Mill dataset between 2021 and 2023 by using the power of data-driven decision-making. Make educated decisions, increase operational efficiency, and unearth useful insights that boost productivity and performance gains. This platform is built to meet your data analysis needs, enabling you to explore, analyze, and extract useful information from mill data to improve your decision-making process.

This interface aims to empower operators, engineers, and researchers by merging expertise and technology to improve the efficiency and production of the Petcoke Mill. We use human expertise, data power, and powerful algorithms to drive continuous improvement and produce optimal results.

About the dataset

The target variables are:

  • Mill Power: Represents the power of the mill motor in kilowatts (KW).
  • Draw Speed: Refers to the speed at which the fan clears the ground petroleum coke in revolutions per minute (rpm).
The input variables for the model are as follows:
  • Parameters related to hot gases:
    • Input Temperature: Pertains to the temperature of the hot gases at the inlet in degrees Celsius (°C).
    • Exhaust air Flow Rate: Represents the flow rate of the hot gases at the inlet in cubic meters per second (m³/s).
  • Parameters related to petcoke:
    • Coal Mill Flow Rate: Represents the input flow rate of petcoke into the mill in cubic meters per second (m³/s).
    • Mill Differential Pressure (ΔP): Refers to the pressure difference between the inlet pressure of the hot gases and the outlet pressure of the mixture (hot gases + ground petcoke). This variable implicitly reflects the petroleum coke content in megapascals (MPa).
  • Parameters related to the process:
    • Transport Chain Speed : Represents the speed of the scraper after separation from the hot gases in revolutions per minute (rpm).
